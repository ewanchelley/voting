Construct the majority graph for the rankings. In the graph, each candidate is represented by a vertex, and a directed edge is drawn between vertices if one candidate is preferred over the other by the majority of voters. A topological ordering of the graph is an ordering of the vertices (candidates), such that for every edge in the graph from candidate A to candidate B, A comes before B in the order.
